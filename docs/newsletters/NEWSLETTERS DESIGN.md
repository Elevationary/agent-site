# Elevationary AI Newsletters

## Overview
This document outlines the standards, processes, and guidelines for the Elevationary AI newsletter program, featuring the 3-2-1 format across 18 specialized editions.

## 3-2-1 Newsletter Format

### Structure
Each newsletter follows this consistent structure:

#### 3 Practical Stories
- Real-world examples of AI application
- Concise case studies with actionable insights
- Focus on measurable outcomes
- Includes source attribution and key takeaways

#### 2 Actionable Insights
- Practical frameworks and strategies
- Ready-to-implement tips
- Department-specific guidance
- Step-by-step instructions when applicable

#### 1 Big Question
- Thought-provoking prompt
- Encourages engagement and reflection
- Ties back to broader business impact
- Includes call-to-action for response

## Content Guidelines

### Tone and Style
- Professional yet approachable
- Clear and concise language
- Active voice preferred
- Use bullet points and short paragraphs
- Include relevant examples and case studies

### Visual Identity
- **Color Palette**:
  - Primary: #2C4773 (Deep navy blue)
  - Accent: #418BCB (Bright sky blue)
  - Secondary: #1E1C32 (Dark indigo)
  - Highlight: #1B5F7F (Teal blue)
- **Logo Requirements**:
  - Must include circuit patterns
  - Department-specific iconography
  - Consistent typography across all editions

### Content Quality Standards
- All facts must be verified
- Sources must be properly attributed
- Avoid technical jargon without explanation
- Ensure accessibility (alt text, proper heading structure)
- Maintain consistent formatting

## Publishing Workflow

### Content Creation Process
1. **Research & Curation** (Day 1-3)
   - Identify trending topics
   - Source relevant case studies
   - Validate information accuracy

2. **Content Generation** (Day 4-5)
   - Draft 3-2-1 content
   - Create visual assets
   - Write meta descriptions and alt text

3. **Review & Approval** (Day 6-7)
   - Editorial review
   - Fact-checking
   - Final approval

4. **Publishing** (Day 8)
   - Format in MailChimp
   - Schedule distribution
   - Publish to website

### Distribution Channels
- Email (primary)
- Website (archive)
- Social media (teasers)
- Subscriber-only content area

## AI Content Generation

### Process
1. **Input Parameters**
   - Newsletter topic
   - Target audience
   - Previous content (to avoid duplication)
   - Performance data from past issues

2. **Generation**
   - Use structured prompts
   - Maintain 3-2-1 format
   - Include relevant examples

3. **Human Review**
   - Fact-checking
   - Tone adjustment
   - Brand alignment
