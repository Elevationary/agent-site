# Elevationary Brand Style Directive

## 1. Source of Truth
The central repository for all brand assets is the **Global Brand Library**:
`~/.gemini/antigravity/library/brand_identity/`

**DO NOT** maintain local copies of logos or PDFs. Retrieve them on demand.

## 2. Accessing Assets
Use the **Brand Manager** skill to find files:
`python3 ~/.gemini/antigravity/skills/brand_manager/scripts/brand_get_asset.py "query"`

## 3. Visual Identity

- **Deep Navy (Brand Core):** `#2C4773`
- **Bright Blue (Secondary):** `#418BCB`

### Typography
- **Primary Standard (Digital/Workspace):** `Roboto`
