*Rules for UI Design, Copywriting, and Asset Creation.*

## 1. Visual Identity
### Color Palette
**Primary Codes:**
- **Deep Navy (Brand Core):** `#2C4773` (R44 G71 B115) - *CMYK: 100/78/29/14*
- **Bright Blue (Secondary):** `#418BCB` (R65 G139 B203) - *CMYK: 86/31/0/0*

**Supporting Palette:**
- **Dark Violet/Black:** `#1E1C32` (R30 G28 B50)
- **Teal/Slate:** `#1B5F7F` (R27 G95 B127)
- **Grey:** `#BCBDBF` (R188 G189 B191)
- **Off-White:** `#F1F2F2` (R241 G242 B242)

### Typography
- **Primary Standard (Digital/Workspace):** `Roboto`
    - **Why:** Excellent density, visual appeal, and universal availability (Google Standard).
    - **Weights:** Regular (Body), Medium (Subheads), Bold (Headlines).
- **Master Brand (Print/Legacy):** `Absolut Pro` 
    - *Note: Only use if specifically required by Creative Director for print assets.*
